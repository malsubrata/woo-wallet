# TeraWallet: Customer Documentation

Welcome to TeraWallet! Your digital wallet makes shopping faster and rewards you for your loyalty.

## 1. Getting Started
Log in to your account and go to the **My Wallet** tab in your dashboard. Here you can see your current balance and manage your funds.

## 2. Wallet Usage
- **Add Balance (Top-up)**: Go to the "Add Amount" tab, enter the amount you want to add, and complete the payment using any available payment method at checkout.
- **Transfer Money**: You can send balance to other users by entering their email address in the "Transfer" tab. Note: Store transfer fees may apply.
- **View Transactions**: The "Transactions" tab shows your full history, including credits (cashback, top-ups) and debits (purchases, transfers).

## 3. Paying with Your Wallet
- **Full Payment**: If your balance is enough, select **TeraWallet** as your payment method at checkout for an instant purchase.
- **Partial Payment**: If your balance is not enough to cover the total, select the "Use Wallet Balance" option at checkout. The system will deduct your balance first, and you can pay the remaining amount with another payment method.

## 4. Earning Rewards
You can earn extra wallet balance by:
- Creating a new account.
- Writing helpful reviews on products you've purchased.
- Visiting the site daily.
- Referring friends who make a purchase.
- Earning cashback on specific orders or items.

## 5. FAQs
- **Q: Where is my cashback?**
  - A: Cashback is added to your wallet once your order is fully processed or completed by the store admin.
- **Q: Can I withdraw my balance to my bank account?**
  - A: TeraWallet is typically for store credit only unless the store has specifically enabled withdrawal features.
- **Q: What happens if I return an item?**
  - A: If you are refunded for an order paid via wallet, the funds will be credited back to your balance automatically.
- **Q: Does my balance expire?**
  - A: TeraWallet balances do not expire unless otherwise stated in the store's terms and conditions.
